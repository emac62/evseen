About

EventsSeen is a new kind of social media platform that has been designed to connect people who have shared similar experiences at live events. This moderated website encourages the sharing of positive and unique ticketed events.

Concerts

Share pictures and stories from your favourite concerts with others who were there. Don't remember the date or the location? Most likely, someone else does. What better way to relive an awesome day!

Sports

Did you see that? We have all said it but may not have shared the experience with those around us. If sports is your thing, grab your tickets, memorabilia and videos to share with others who saw it too.

Theatre

When the curtain rises, the show is a unique moment for only that audience. Share your memories with other like-minded people and return to that moment when your heart soared and your senses heightened.

My EventsSeen

Control the events that you want to share with this homebase. You will be able to store photos and post events that interest you.

Frenatics

Friends are the best when they have information and stories to add to your knowledge. Follow people and have them follow you to learn more about your favourite events. With one another we can expand our understanding of a favourite moment.
