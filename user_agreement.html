EventsSeen Rules

This is a social website meant to share authentic ticketed moments and, as such, will follow standard social mores.

Explicitly:

  • use your real name and be proud to share your viewpoint. Don’t hide or pretend to be someone else. Accounts found to be inauthentic will be terminated
  • be kind to others. Don’t diminish another person’s memory or experience. We are here to build on each other’s awesome moments
  • no harassment. Attacking people in hopes of anonymity is not acceptable. This Site is for sharing and will not tolerate derogatory comments
  • no violent, pornographic or sexually explicit images, links or content is allowed. Linking to an event to this type of negativity incites and frightens. It will be removed and your account will be flagged. Once an account has been flagged three times, it will be terminated
  • no fake information. While we appreciate the nature of memory, the facts surrounding an event must not be changed to mislead or confuse. We encourage additional details and will remove what is factually untrue
  • respect another person’s experience. As each moment is unique, so is one’s response to it. Stay on the topic of the event and avoid interactions that fixate on a person’s reaction. It is in our different perspectives that this website becomes powerful and exciting. Don’t kill the fun and keep a positive tone
  • finally, EventsSeen uses a report and investigate algorithm. Comments and content that is meant to enflame, behaviour that harasses overtly or subtlety, and people identified as bad actors will be flagged. This flag is visible on a person’s profile page and can only be removed by EventsSeen. This allows the Website to alert users to a person’s past behaviour. When three flags are reached, the account will be shut down
